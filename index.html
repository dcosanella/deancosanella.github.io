About Me

In December 2015, I received a Bachelor of Science Degree in Computer Science from California State University, San Bernardino. This is one of my greatest acheivements so far in life. I am currently looking to begin my career as a software developer. Software development is something that I have been very passionate about since I started studying and learning about it in 2012. I have tried to learn as many different programming languages as I can so I will be able to branch out into different fields of software development. On my projects page, you will be able to see that I have tried to create different types of projects to show that I am capable of working on all different types of projects and applications.

Something that I struggle with on a daily basis is my speech impediment. I have a stutter when I talk and it makes it very difficult for me to interview for jobs and meet new people. Over the years, I have learned to control my stuttering but I still have a long way to go to be fluent like most people. I know that my stuttering can give people a negative first impression of me when they meet me for the first time. But when people get to know me, they find out that I am a normal person just like everyone else who just has some trouble speaking sometimes. Because of my stutter, I work twice as hard on every project that I work on because I feel like I need to make up for my lack of oral communication skills. I am an extremely hard worker and I am very confident in my abilities as a developer. I know that I can make a positive contribution to anyone who is willing to give me an opportunity.
